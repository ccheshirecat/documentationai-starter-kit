## Overview

HYPR makes it easy to build and manage your documentation. Follow these steps to create your account, set up a workspace, and import your first content. You will have a live documentation site ready in under 5 minutes.

## Create Your Account

Start by signing up for HYPR. You need an email address and password.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit HYPR" icon="globe">
    Go to `https://app.hyprdocs.com` and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="edit-3">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and verify your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Setup" icon="check-circle">
    Accept the terms and access your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Use a business email for team invites later. You can upgrade to a paid plan anytime from the dashboard.
</Callout>

## Set Up Your Workspace

Create a new documentation space for your project.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="New Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, click **New Workspace**.

    1. Enter a name like `My Project Docs`.
    2. Choose a subdomain such as `myproject.hyprdocs.com`.
    3. Select a theme or use the default blue (`#3B82F6`).

    Your workspace is ready to publish.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Team Workspace" icon="users">
    Invite team members during setup.

    Add emails in the **Team** section. They receive invite links.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Import Initial Content

Populate your workspace with content. HYPR supports Markdown files, GitHub repos, and direct uploads.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="GitHub Repo" icon="github">
    Connect your repo for automatic syncs.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Authorize GitHub">
        Click **Import from GitHub** and grant access.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Repo">
        Choose `your-username/my-docs-repo`.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Configure Branch">
        Set `main` as the default branch.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Markdown Upload" icon="upload-cloud">
    Drag and drop files directly.

    Create a folder structure like this:

    ```mermaid
    graph TD
        A[Workspace Root] --> B[docs]
        B --> C[quickstart.mdx]
        B --> D[introduction.mdx]
        A --> E[public]
        E --> F[image.png]
    ```

    Upload your `README.md` as the homepage.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
